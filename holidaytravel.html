Uk Farm

UK Farm is an agricultural attraction located in Kluang, Johor, Malaysia. It is a farm that combines ecology, leisure and food and is know for its rich ecological experiences, offering activities such as feeding alpacas, walking and making your own ice cream. It is a great place for families to explore nature.

Highlands

Cameron Highlands is a famous summer resort located in Pahang, Malaysia, know for its tea plantations, refreshing climate, strawberry farms, vegetable gardens and farms, while also being famous for its architecture that blends colonial-era features.
