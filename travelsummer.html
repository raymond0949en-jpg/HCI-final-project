Langkawi
Cenang Beach

Located in Langkawi, Malaysia, the beach is renowned for its fine white sand and azure waters. The beach offers stunning sunset views and a glimpse into the island's relaxed lifestyle. It also provides a range of water activities such as banana boating and parasailing.

Penang
ESCAPE Theme Park

Escape Penang boasts the longest water slide in Malaysia and features three main areas: Adventure Park. It also offers 30 land-based activities such as ziplining, climbing challenges, and high-altitude jumps.
